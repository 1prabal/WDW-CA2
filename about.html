About Me

Hello! I am Prabal Gupta, a computer science student at KIET Group of Institution with a strong interest in front-end development and user-centered design. I enjoy transforming complex ideas into simple, beautiful interfaces that feel effortless to use.

My curiosity keeps me experimenting with new technologies, frameworks, and design tools. Outside coursework, I participate in hackathons and collaborate on open-source projects to sharpen my skills and learn from others.

When I am not coding, you will find me sketching interface ideas, exploring local coffee shops, or mentoring classmates who are just getting started in tech.

I aspire to grow into a product designer who champions accessibility and small, delightful details in every experience I create.
